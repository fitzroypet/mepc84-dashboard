Setup Required

Connect your Google Sheets to power this dashboard:

  1. Open your MEPC84_Stance_Analysis_v2.xlsx in Google Drive (it auto-converts to Sheets)
  2. Share it as "Anyone with the link can view"
  3. Copy the Sheet ID from the URL: docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/[ID HERE]/edit
  4. Repeat for UN_IMO_Country_Development_Classification.xlsx
  5. Paste both IDs into js/config.js and reload

Until configured, the dashboard loads from the local Excel files as a fallback.
